Questions to ask your mentor

Adapting to a new country while planning your career can be challenging. Our expert resources, mentorship programs, and strategic guidance equip you with the knowledge and skills you need to excel.

  • How did you shortlist universities and programs?
  • What were the biggest challenges you faced as an international student?
  • How did you manage your finances, including tuition and living expenses?
  • What was the visa process like, and do you have any tips?
  • Did you face any culture shock? How did you deal with it?
  • How did you build your network and make friends in a new country?
